Our Services

Customer Protection Review Callouts

Each month we will call on your current BOB to set up CPR’s. If they wish to set up a review they will be directed to your office to do so. If they wish to decline or a message was left we will document each call and send at the end of each month.

Comprehensive CPRs Reports

We will send a report stating which customers accepted the review, which ones declined, and which ones we left a message on. It will show the date and the exact time that we called. These are records you can keep to protect you from exposures and liabilities you may face for coverage issues.

Survey Call Reminders

We all know how important surveys are and how they can impact our compensation. We will call each of your customers around the time they receive their surveys to remind them to please fill them out. We will give them an option that if they cannot fill them out in a satisfactory manner to please speak to your agent first. They will then be directed to you to hopefully work out the issue in advance.

Holiday Calls

We will call your entire book of business to wish them happy holidays. You can pick as many or as few of holidays as you want. It is completely up to you.

Welcome Calls

Each month you can upload you previous month’s new business and we will call to welcome them to your agency. At this point if they have any questions or did not receive any documents they can be directed to your office.

Catastrophe Call Outs (coming soon)

Did you have a major storm come through your area? Well you can get on our site and at one click of a mouse send a call out to your entire BOB letting them know that you hope everyone is alright and that you are here to help with anything they need.

Thank You Calls

We all know how hectic and busy it can get in our agencies. How often do we just sit back and say Thank you to our customers. Well we will make sure that gets done for you. As many times as you like we will call your current BOB and tell them Thank You for being a part of Allstate and your agency.

At the time of setup we will have you record each of these messages so it is your voice the customer hears. We have the scripts for you or you can come up with your own. It only needs to be done once and takes about 5 minutes. You can change them as often as you’d like by calling 1-866-827-1716
